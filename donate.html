My family and I have been using paysend.com to transfer funds directly to cards. Paysend is secure (PCI DSS certified), has low fees, and all transfers are sent in real time. Wise, or TransferWise, has also been a great program and has the same qualifications. Wire transfers through the bank have a significantly longer wait time, and many of them have exorbitant fees. Both of these are directly antithetical to our goals for the fundraising, as this is a developing and ongoing immediate crisis.

If you would like to donate directly, please contact me and I will provide the information of the individual you would like to donate to. I do not have their information listed below to prevent widespread knowledge of their card information to diminish the chance of card fraud. If you choose to donate directly, the only issue I have experienced with Paysend and Wise was my bank requiring confirmation (as a fraud protection measure) for the first transfer.
