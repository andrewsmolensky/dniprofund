Hello! My name is Kate Slinchenkova and I am a senior getting a double major at Cornell in Biology and Chemistry. I grew up in Dnipro, Ukraine before moving to America in 2008, leaving the majority of my family back in Ukraine. After moving to America, I have met my boyfriend, Andrew Smolensky, who’s family is also from Dnipro. He is currently an Electrical Engineering student at Purdue University and is the mastermind behind the website design. Although we both used to visit often, now we will not be able to return due to the current attacks. To help, we have spent most of our free time and date nights helping our loved ones and their efforts in supporting the community the only way we can from so far away: financially.
