Hello! My name is Kate Slinchenkova and I am a senior getting a double major at Cornell in Biology and Chemistry. I grew up in Dnipro, Ukraine before moving to America in 2008, leaving the majority of my family back in Ukraine. Although I used to visit often, now I will not be able to return due to the current attacks. To help, I have spent most of my free time helping my loved ones and their efforts in supporting the community the only way I can from so far away: financially.



Thank you for your interest in supporting my friends and family in their efforts in Ukraine! Please make your way to the tabs in the upper menu for more information. You will find information about who I am transferring donations to in the “About the People” tab and you will find pictures and receipts of purchases in the “Updates” tab. Please feel free to contact me with any questions.
